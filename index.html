Welcome to Flobot!

Flobot has been developed as an alternative for organizations (especially non-profits and Government departments) who need modern and continually-evolving back-office software - but can do without the complexity, expense and vendor lock-in that usually comes with it.

By placing an emphasis on openness, collaboration and self-sufficiency, Flobot has been specifically engineered to deliver a service that can meet the challenges facing today’s Public Sector.

Flobot is a low-code development framework for Node.js.
